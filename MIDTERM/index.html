Anemo

The element of wind, representing freedom and movement. Anemo users can manipulate wind to create powerful effects.

Geo

The element of earth, symbolizing strength and resilience. Geo users create protective barriers and summon rock constructs.

Pyro

The element of fire, representing passion and destruction. Pyro users unleash flames to deal heavy damage.

Cryo

The element of ice, symbolizing calm and persistence. Cryo users freeze enemies and create icy attacks.

Hydro

The element of water, representing life and healing. Hydro users manipulate water for support and offense.

Electro

The element of electricity, symbolizing energy and speed. Electro users summon lightning for swift attacks.

Dendro

The element of nature, symbolizing growth and wisdom. Dendro users manipulate plants for unique elemental reactions.
